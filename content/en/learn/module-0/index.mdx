## Cultured Conversation

Meaningful conversation can only take place in a culture of respect and courtesy, between people 
who are capable of seeing their highest ideals in others and who can recognise and reflect the 
essential sameness which lies at the heart of each and every human being. We tend to only engage 
in conversations which we think we can "take something away from", be it new knowledge, an 
altered perspective, or an inside story we can leverage for our own gain.  

However, Kernel conversations are about *humility*, *presence* and a *genuine attentiveness* 
towards others, not because you stand to benefit from them, but because cultivating these three 
capacities within yourself is its own reward, which may allow you to see clearly the complete 
and perfect sacred core of every person you encounter.
