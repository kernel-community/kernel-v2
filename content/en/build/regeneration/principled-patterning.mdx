# Principled Patterning

## Purpose

> As we build this consensus economy, it‚Äôs our privilege and responsibility to deeply consider the design patterns, tools and language we adopt.

As our beloved Cryptowanderer says, human beings are moral creatures who learn. We have the unique ability to *intend* ‚Äî¬†to conceive of the consequences of our actions, and organize ourselves to realize certain outcomes. This is the essence of coordination. We find mutual agreement (somehow), and work together to realize a vision of some future state. 

The regeneration movement holds the vision to foster a societal transformation so that all humans can thrive in harmony with  Earth. We are not here to "save the world". The world will be fine. Instead, the depth and breadth of life on Earth is at stake.

Our task is to envision the future we want to see realized ‚Äî¬†the timeline we want to play out on Earth ‚Äî¬†and discern what we each can do, now, to maximize the likelihood of it coming to pass.

> Regeneration puts life at the center of every action and decision.

## Objectives

Through the Regeneration Guild, we will: 

1. Surface a holistic, multidimensional definition of "regeneration", honoring each perspective and understanding that we will hold different value-sets ‚Äî¬†finding intersubjective consensus,
2. Delve into our system of systems and identify key leverage points that hold exceptional potential to catalyze a rapid, graceful transition to a regenerative paradigm,
3. Reflect on our own "loci of control" ‚Äî¬†what is within our power to change.

As an outcome, we hope to make a bit more sense of what a regenerative economy will look like and outline a credible path to bringing it about, aided by tools being built on the open web.
